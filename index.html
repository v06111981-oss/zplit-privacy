1. Who We Are

Zplit is a bill-splitting application for Android. By using Zplit, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect — and Where It Stays

DataPurposeWhere it's stored
Your display nameShown to people at your table during a sessionOn your device only (SharedPreferences)

We do not collect: location data, device identifiers, analytics, crash reports, or any data that is transmitted to our servers — because we have no servers.

3. Permissions Explained

📷 Camera
Used exclusively to scan receipt text via on-device ML Kit OCR. Images are never saved to storage or uploaded anywhere.

📶 Bluetooth & Wi-Fi Direct (Nearby Connections)
Used for the "Table session" feature which connects devices in the same room without internet. Communication is peer-to-peer and encrypted by Google Nearby Connections API. No data leaves your local network. This feature uses Google Play Services.

👤 Contacts (READ_CONTACTS)
Optional. Used only when you choose to add people from your contacts list. Contact data is used in-memory and never uploaded.

4. Third-Party Services

Google ML Kit (Text Recognition)
Processes camera frames on-device for OCR. No images are sent to Google servers. ML Kit Terms

Google Nearby Connections API
Used for offline peer-to-peer table sessions. Requires Google Play Services. Google Privacy Policy

Google Play Billing
Used for the one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited scans. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. Google Payments Privacy Notice

5. Data Retention

Your display name is stored in SharedPreferences on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app. No other data is retained between sessions.

6. Children's Privacy

Zplit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy. The date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at: zplit.app@gmail.com
